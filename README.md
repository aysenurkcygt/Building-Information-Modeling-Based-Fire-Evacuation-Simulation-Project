# Building Information Modeling Based Fire Evacuation Simulation-Project

The Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey (TÜBİTAK) funded Project

2021 - 2024

## My Role
I am involved as a scientific researcher, contributing to:
- Designing and implementing fire and evacuation scenarios,
- Integrating BIM data into simulation workflows,
- Performing spatial analyses to support emergency planning and improve building safety
